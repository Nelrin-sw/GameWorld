Smite

Smite is a free-to-play, third-person multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game developed and
published by Hi-Rez Studios for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox One. In Smite,
players control a god, goddess, or other mythological figure, and take part in team-based combat,
using their abilities and tactics against other player-controlled gods and non-player-controlled minions.

The game has multiple player versus player (PVP) modes, many playable characters, and has a successful
esports scene with multiple tournaments, including the annual million-dollar Smite World Championship.
